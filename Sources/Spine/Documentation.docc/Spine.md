# ``Spine``

This Swift library allows you to upload characters and their animations from the [Spine (ESS version)](http://esotericsoftware.com) (v4.1+) to `SpriteKit` for platforms: `iOS(8.0+)`, `macOS(10.10+)`, `tvOS(9.0+)`, `watchOS(3.0+)`
